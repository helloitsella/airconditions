Air Conditions 2021 is gathered and cared for by a group of collaborators representing work in a wide variety of practices and mediums from sound, choir compositions, music, writing, drama and visual works to academic talks, interviews and readings of theory - all focused on the subject, substance and discourse of Air in a radio-based format.
